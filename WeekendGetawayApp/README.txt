Weekend Getaway App

An app that makes it very easy to book a weekend at a hotel/spa/resort.
You open the app and there's a list of offers for you to enjoy.
You choose which one you want you click on "book now" and enter the necessary data required.
You may cancel your reservation for free if it's not within a week of realization.
The travel is already arranged all you need to do is show up on the settled location.
